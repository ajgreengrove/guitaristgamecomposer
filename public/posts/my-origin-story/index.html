Now the clarifying story in more natural language. I have a classical background and love early music, so over time I’ll delve deeper into a genre combination of progressive rock sound with baroque & game music influence.

The John Dowland reference is a joke about the command line which I literally live in, and Henry Purcell’s Abdelaizer was used in Thunder Castle (1986).

And then for the more PITA part: I started as A J Greengrove, but after having to learn about business, marketing etc, realized that people just wanted A J Greengrove to play guitar, not to do all this game stuff. One brand name = one avatar (audience). Thus ~$ GgC, “coding gamedev composer”, was born.
